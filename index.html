We're all part of Satoshi's mission...

We are all Satoshi

Just as Bitcoin is a Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System, it is also a Peer-to-Peer social system.

It is up to each and every one of us to share this goal with others. A truly grassroots movement can help ensure the roots dig deep and hold, rather than being the next shallow hyped trend.

How do we accomplish this? As easy as 1, 2, 3.

1


Help someone set up a wallet and send them some sats!

2


Connect with them -- find out what matters most to them!

3


Demonstrate how can this be used today and what's possible tomorrow!

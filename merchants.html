How can I begin accepting BCH?

First, it is important to decide which method is right for you!

Self-Custody

Control your payments directly with a self-custody system, keeping your funds secure and independent.

  • Full Control: You directly manage your BCH funds, free from third-party access.

  • High Security: Proper key management protects funds from hacks or service failures.

  • No Fees: Avoid payment processor fees to keep more revenue.

  • Tradeoff: Requires technical know-how and secure key management, with no recovery if keys are lost.

Payment Processor

Simplify your transactions with a payment provider, offering easy management and added support.

  • User-Friendly: Can simplify BCH payments with more familiar interfaces.

  • Extra Services: Includes fraud protection, support, and system integrations.

  • Quick Setup: Start accepting BCH payments without technical expertise.

  • Tradeoff: Involves fees and risks from relying on a third party.
